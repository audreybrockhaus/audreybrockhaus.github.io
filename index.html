Currently planning 2018 CSS Day

What happened to 2017 CSS Day?

After thoughtful consideration, the organizing team has come to the decision to cancel CSS Day 2017. While we are all disappointed by this, we felt it better to cancel than to put on a conference that did not live up to the high expectations of ourselves and the greater Phoenix developer community. We sincerely apologize for any inconvenience this may have caused.

Now for the good news: this decision will allow the organizing team to focus on making CSS Day even better in 2018. If you want to help ensure our success next year, contact info@cssday.io to get involved.
